Adventure Awaits

Experience the thrill of the river with the most trusted guides in the industry. Our team is dedicated to providing safety and excitement for every guest, regardless of skill level.

Our History

Since 1995, we have been dedicated to providing the most thrilling and safe whitewater rafting experiences. What started with a single raft on the Salmon River has grown into a premier adventure company.

Under the leadership of Brain Victor Murara, we continue to expand our reach, offering more trips and better equipment every year to ensure every guest has a world-class adventure.
