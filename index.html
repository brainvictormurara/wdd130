Discover Zimbabwe

Zimbabwe is a land of unmatched natural beauty and ancient mystery. At the heart of our tourism is the legendary Victoria Falls (Mosi-oa-Tunya), the world's largest curtain of falling water. Standing at the edge of the rainforest, one can experience the "Smoke that Thunders" as the mighty Zambezi River plunges into the Batoka Gorge.

For those drawn to history, the Great Zimbabwe National Monument offers a glimpse into a medieval city that was once the capital of a vast empire. The mortarless stone walls stand as a testament to the architectural genius of our ancestors.

Adventure continues at the Chinhoyi Caves, where the cobalt-blue "Sleeping Pool" reaches depths of over 100 meters, offering crystal-clear waters that never change temperature. From the balancing rocks of Epworth to the mist-covered mountains of the Eastern Highlands, Zimbabwe is truly the jewel of Africa.
